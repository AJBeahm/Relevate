Our Mission

To bring research-informed, personalized and up-to-date information to young adults to help them achieve and maintain healthy romantic relationships through accessible mobile technology.

Why?

  • Greater access to relationship education for all
    • Low cost
    • Easily accessible(via computer or phone)
    • Self-guided & adaptive
  • 42% of young adults already use smart phones to access health or medical information (Fox & Duggan, 2012).
  • Premarital education associated with higher marital quality and lower odds of divorce (Stanley et al., 2006)
  • 30 hrs. 15 min.: average amount of time per month adults over 18 are using mobile phone apps (Nielson, 2014)
  • 80% of young adults today own a smart phone (Duggan & Brenner, 2013).
